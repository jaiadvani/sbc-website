What is it?

The Sarvatobhadra Chakra

"Auspicious in All Directions" — a sacred 9×9 cosmological matrix that maps the entire universe of Vedic influence.

The Sarvatobhadra Chakra is one of the most sophisticated and versatile instruments in the entire corpus of Vedic astrology. Unlike conventional charts, the SBC integrates nakshatras (lunar mansions), Sanskrit phonemes, weekdays, and rashis (zodiac signs) into a unified cosmological grid of extraordinary depth.

Its central mechanism — "vedha" or piercing — reveals how transiting planets interact with the sensitive points in an individual's cosmic blueprint, offering predictions about health, relationships, timing, travel, and destiny with remarkable precision.

Guruji's Journey

"The Sarvatobhadra Chakra is not merely a predictive tool — it is a mirror of cosmic consciousness, reflecting the divine order that permeates all existence."

Guruji's journey with the Sarvatobhadra Chakra began under the tutelage of his own Guru in the sacred tradition of classical Jyotisha. Having immersed himself in the primary Sanskrit texts — including the Brihat Samhita, Sarvatobhadra texts, and rare manuscript traditions — he brings decades of rigorous scholarship to every consultation.

What distinguishes Guruji's approach is his rare ability to bridge the precision of classical methodology with compassionate, practical guidance for modern seekers. He neither sensationalizes predictions nor reduces this profound science to mere fortune-telling, but uses it as a tool for genuine self-understanding and life navigation.

Having consulted thousands of individuals across India and internationally, Guruji continues to research, teach, and share the living tradition of Sarvatobhadra Chakra through consultations, workshops, and his growing library of articles and video lectures.
